How it works
01

Start a game

Create a match and share the invite link with a friend via WhatsApp, iMessage, or a QR code.

02

Submit your word

Each round, you and your friend secretly submit a word. No peeking until you've both answered.

03

Match = you win!

If your words match, you win! If not, see both words and use them as hints for the next round.
